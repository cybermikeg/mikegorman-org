My Introduction

Over the years I have created many websites to advertise the work I was currently involved in, from IT contracting to skydive courses. This website is really now just about me, and comes form the fact that you can now have actual digitial identities online.

The fact we now have a decentralised version of the internet I find fascinating, and returns more control back to the normal person in my opinion. Especially when it seems that there is more and more corruption at higher levels, and appears these are the people that wish to have more and more control.

I also find cryptocurrenies fascinating and hopefully the world can now discover sound money, and not currencies that are being devalued by constant money printing within economies where the debt level are just spiralling out of control.
